## About

I've been writing software for over a decade — long enough to have seen trends rise and fade, frameworks come and go, and countless architectural debates settle themselves in production.

What's remained constant: the value of clean, maintainable code that solves real problems for real users.

I focus on building end-to-end systems that are reliable under load, straightforward to maintain, and honest about their trade-offs. I care less about what's new and more about what works. My preference is always to ship something solid and iterate, rather than architect indefinitely and deliver late.

- 12+ years of experience across the full software delivery lifecycle
- Comfortable moving between frontend polish and backend infrastructure
- Strong preference for clean architecture and separation of concerns
- Believe that the best systems are the ones teams can confidently change six months later
